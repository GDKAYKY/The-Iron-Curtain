Carved by stunning fjords, Norway is a land of rugged beauty. Bergen, its beating heart, is a port city buzzing with trade in fish and timber. The powerful nobility hold sway, but life for most Norwegians revolves around the sea, with fishing and farming forming the backbone of the economy. The harsh yet breathtaking landscape shapes their way of life. Looking ahead, Norway's future rests on its ability to harness its natural resources and carve out its own path on the world stage.
