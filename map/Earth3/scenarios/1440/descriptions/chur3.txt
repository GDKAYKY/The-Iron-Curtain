Chur, a city nestled amidst the Swiss Alps, is the seat of a powerful bishop. As a Prince-Bishop, the ruler of Chur holds significant temporal and spiritual authority. The city's strategic location at the crossroads of Alpine passes has made it a vital trade hub. However, its mountainous surroundings and the constant threat of invasion from neighboring powers pose challenges to its prosperity and independence. Despite these obstacles, Chur maintains its status as a regional center of power and influence.
