Masovia exists in a state of constant change. Formerly a powerful duchy, it's now a shadow of its former self. Despite this, remnants of Masovia's past linger. The Dukes of Masovia still hold some local influence, residing in grand castles scattered throughout the region. The fertile plains are dotted with farms and villages, producing a steady agricultural output. However, tensions simmer with the Teutonic Knights, a rival power to the north.
