The once-unified Shiba find themselves at a crossroads. Internal disputes cast a shadow over their illustrious past. Grand halls echo with hushed arguments, strategies clash. In their ancestral provinces, some Shiba hold fast to tradition, while others yearn for change. Loyal samurai train diligently, their blades sharp but their purpose uncertain. The Shiba, a force with a rich history, must navigate the treacherous currents of internal politics and forge a new path to reclaim their former glory.
