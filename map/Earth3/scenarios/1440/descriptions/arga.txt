Armagnac is a Gascon county in southwestern France, known for its proud and independent spirit. The region has a rich history marked by both prosperity and conflict. While under the French crown, Armagnac maintains a degree of autonomy and retains a strong regional identity. The county's fertile lands contribute to its agricultural wealth, while its strategic location offers opportunities for trade and commerce. However, the region is also susceptible to the challenges of borderland life, including incursions from neighboring powers and the demands of the French monarchy.
