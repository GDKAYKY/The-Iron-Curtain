Worms stands as a prominent imperial city on the Rhine River. Its strategic location at the crossroads of trade routes contributes to its economic prosperity. Renowned for its imperial diets and rich history, the city exudes an air of significance. As a free imperial city, Worms enjoys a degree of autonomy while navigating the complex web of Holy Roman Empire politics. Its commitment to trade, craftsmanship, and culture underpins its status as a regional powerhouse.
