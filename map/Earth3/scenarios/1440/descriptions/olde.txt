Oldenburg is a rising power in northwestern Germany, ruled by the ambitious Count Dietrich. Strategically located near the North Sea, the town thrives on trade. Merchants gather in its market squares, while its harbor welcomes ships laden with goods. Oldenburg is known for its skilled metalworkers, whose craftsmanship produces coveted tools and weapons. The imposing Oldenburg Castle dominates the skyline, a symbol of the Count's growing influence. Though a young player on the regional scene, Oldenburg bristles with potential, poised for future prominence.
