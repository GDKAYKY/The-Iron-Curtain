Landshut, a bustling Bavarian town, thrives under the rule of Duke Henry IV. A center of trade, it sits along the Isar River, a vital transport route. The town boasts impressive Gothic architecture, with the imposing Burg Trausnitz, the Duke's residence, a prominent landmark. Religious life flourishes with Saints Martin and Corbinian Church standing as a testament to faith. Craftsmanship thrives within the town walls, while merchants ply their wares, bringing prosperity to Landshut.
