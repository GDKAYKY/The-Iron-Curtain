A fortified town nestled on the shores of Lake Constance. Imposing walls guard a bustling market square, where merchants hawk wares from across the region. Skilled artisans ply their trades in workshops, crafting goods for trade and the keep of the powerful Counts of Montfort. Fishermen cast their nets on the lake, a vital source of food and commerce. The majestic mountains that rise nearby provide a sense of both beauty and looming threat. Life in Bregenz revolves around trade, tradition, and loyalty to the Counts, all while keeping a watchful eye on the ever-changing power dynamics around the lake.
