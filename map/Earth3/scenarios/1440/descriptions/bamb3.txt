A prince-bishopric radiates power and piety. The magnificent cathedral dominates the skyline, a testament to the bishop's authority. Cobbled streets wind past bustling markets and skilled artisans' workshops. University halls echo with scholarly debate. Brewers produce their famous beer, filling taverns with laughter and hearty meals. Loyalty to the Church and the bishop is paramount. Bamberg thrives on a blend of religious fervor, intellectual pursuit, and a vibrant trade scene.
