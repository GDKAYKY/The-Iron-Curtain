A bustling Free Imperial City on the trade routes of Europe. Towers, built by wealthy merchants, pierce the sky. Guilds hold sway, ensuring quality and fair trade in everything from textiles to spices. Workshops hum with the industry of metalworkers, printers, and artists. The famed Fugger family begins its rise to prominence, banking on the city's prosperity. Scholars gather at the university, while a magnificent cathedral provides spiritual solace. Augsburg is a city of ambition, innovation, and a fierce sense of independence.
