A fortified town on the Oder River, a crucial trade hub in Silesia. Merchant houses with stepped gables line the bustling market square. The imposing castle, seat of the Piast Dukes, watches over the town. Craftsmen in workshops hammer out metal goods, while weavers produce renowned Glogau linen. The air hums with the activity of guilds, each with its own traditions and secrets. Scholars debate at the collegiate church, fostering a spirit of learning. Glogau thrives on its strategic location and the wealth generated by trade, making it a vital center of commerce and culture in the region.
