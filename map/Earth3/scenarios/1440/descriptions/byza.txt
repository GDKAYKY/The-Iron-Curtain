The Byzantine Empire, once a beacon of Roman civilization, now exists as a mere shadow of its former self. Besieged by the encroaching Ottoman Turks, the empire struggles to maintain its sovereignty. Constantinople, its heart, feels the constant threat of invasion. Despite its rich history and cultural heritage, the empire faces severe economic challenges and internal strife. The weight of the past and the uncertainty of the future converge, demanding resolute leadership and strategic brilliance to stave off oblivion.
