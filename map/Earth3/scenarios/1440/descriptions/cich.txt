A land of warriors and fishermen. Fortresses cling to rugged coastlines, watchful against pirates. Rice paddies shimmer in the valleys, while skilled fishermen cast their nets in the bountiful seas. The Chiba, fiercely independent and loyal, navigate a complex web of alliances. Their swords are sharp, their resolve unwavering. They stand ready to defend their lands and carve their own path in the ever-shifting power struggles of Japan.
