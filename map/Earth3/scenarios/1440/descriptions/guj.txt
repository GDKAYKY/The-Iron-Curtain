Gujarat basks in Arabian Sea trade. Cambay hums with merchants from across the Indian Ocean, exchanging spices, textiles, and Gujarat's famed textiles and dyes. Despite Sultanate rule, Rajput chiefs hold power in their forts. The fading Delhi Sultanate and rising Bengal Sultanate create a tense political climate. Gujarat's future hinges on navigating this complexity to secure its status as a trading and cultural powerhouse.
