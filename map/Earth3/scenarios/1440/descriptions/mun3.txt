Munich is a fledgling Bavarian town on the rise, ruled by the ambitious Wittelsbachs. Ideally situated on trade routes, merchants bring wealth and a steady flow of goods. The impressive Frauenkirche is under construction, a testament to the city's growing importance. Munich's brewers are already laying the foundation for their future fame, crafting beers that will soon be renowned. Though young, Munich brims with potential, hinting at the grand city it will become.
