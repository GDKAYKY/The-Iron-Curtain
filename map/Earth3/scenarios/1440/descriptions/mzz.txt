Mainz, is a powerful city on the Rhine River. A prestigious archbishopric rules the city, making it a center of both religious and political influence. Grand cathedral dominates the skyline. Bustling harbor welcomes merchants from across Europe, while workshops hum with the activity of skilled craftsmen. Universities draw scholars, fostering a vibrant intellectual scene. The spirit of invention hangs in the air, with workshops pushing the boundaries of knowledge. Mainz is a city of wealth, culture, and power.
