Montferrat is a strategic marquisate in northern Italy, bordering Savoy and Milan. Its location has made it a crucial player in the complex political landscape of the region. The Marquisate has historically balanced its relations with its powerful neighbors, seeking to maintain its independence while leveraging its strategic position. With a rich agricultural economy and a burgeoning trade network, Montferrat aims to prosper amidst the constant power struggles of the Italian peninsula.
