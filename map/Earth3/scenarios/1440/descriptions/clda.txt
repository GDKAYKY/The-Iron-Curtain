The Date dragon stirs in the mountainous north. Their formidable castle, a symbol of strength, claws its way up a strategic peak. Within, ambition burns bright in the eyes of the young clan leader. Date warriors, honed by the harsh climate, train relentlessly, their blades flashing like winter lightning. Rumors of their prowess and hunger for expansion spread like wildfire. The southern plains, ripe for conquest, lie in their sights. The Date, a force to be reckoned with, wait for the opportune moment to strike and claim their rightful place in the ever-shifting power struggle of Japan.
