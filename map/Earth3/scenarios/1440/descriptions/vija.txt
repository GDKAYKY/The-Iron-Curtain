Vijayanagar, a South Indian powerhouse, reigns supreme. Its opulent capital boasts magnificent temples and bustling markets. A strong dynasty leads a vast empire, with fearsome warriors and rich trade routes. However, whispers of dissent and a looming northern rival, the Bahmani Sultanate, threaten their dominance. Vijayanagar's future hinges on unity, military strength, and navigating a complex Deccan landscape.
