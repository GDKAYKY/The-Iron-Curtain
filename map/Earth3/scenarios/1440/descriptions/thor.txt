Theodoro clings on as a beacon of Byzantine culture in a changing Crimea. This tiny Greek Orthodox state, nestled in the Mangup fortress high above the surrounding mountains, is a shadow of its former glory. Despite its formidable defenses, Theodoro faces growing pressure from the expanding Crimean Khanate. Inside the fortified walls, a proud people maintain their traditions, clinging to their faith and way of life. The future of Theodoro is uncertain. Can they maintain their independence through diplomacy or martial prowess, or will they be swallowed by their powerful neighbors?
