Rothenburg stands as a prominent city within the Holy Roman Empire. Its strategic location at the crossroads of trade routes contributes to its economic prosperity. Renowned for its fortifications and thriving textile industry, the city attracts merchants and craftsmen alike. As a free imperial city, Rothenburg enjoys a degree of autonomy while navigating the complex web of imperial politics. Its commitment to trade, craftsmanship, and civic governance underpins its status as a regional powerhouse.
