Lan Xang faces a period of uncertainty. The recent death of King Lan Kham Daeng has plunged the kingdom into political turmoil. Multiple rulers have ascended the throne and been swiftly removed, with whispers of a powerful queen, Maha Devi, influencing events behind the scenes. Despite the instability, the capital, Muang Sua, maintains control, quelling a recent rebellion in Vientiane. Lan Xang's future hinges on navigating this period of intrigue and potentially emerging stronger. Buddhist traditions remain central to society, with grand temples adorning the landscape. Trade with neighboring states flourishes, bringing wealth and cultural exchange. Lan Xang's skilled artisans craft exquisite goods, adding to the kingdom's prestige.
