King Binnya Dhamma Yaza leads a bustling kingdom where Burmese, Mon, and Shan cultures interweave. The Irrawaddy River fuels trade, with Pegu famed for its rice, lacquerware, and metalwork. Theravada Buddhism shapes society, with grand pagodas and revered monks. Pegu's military might secures its position as a regional power. Look for Pegu's continued growth and dominance.
