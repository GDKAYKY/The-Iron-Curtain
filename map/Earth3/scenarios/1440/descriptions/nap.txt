Naples stands as a resplendent gem of southern Italy. Ruled by the Angevin dynasty, the city basks in a rich cultural heritage and economic prosperity. Its strategic coastal position and fertile hinterland fuel a thriving trade network. As a center of art, learning, and diplomacy, Naples exerts significant influence throughout the region. Yet, the city's grandeur is often shadowed by the complexities of Italian politics, demanding constant vigilance to protect its sovereignty and prosperity.
