Osel Wiek, a necklace of islands off the Estonian coast, navigates a complex path. Ruled by a Bishop, the archipelago bristles with a yearning for independence among its inhabitants. The strategic island of Saaremaa holds the key, a valuable outpost for trade and defense. Impregnable castles dot the landscape, but local leaders also wield significant power. Osel Wiek's future hinges on finding a balance between outside authority and the islanders' desire to chart their own course.
