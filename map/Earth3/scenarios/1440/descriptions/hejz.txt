Hejaz stands as the sacred heartland of Islam, encompassing the holy cities of Mecca and Medina. Its religious significance draws pilgrims from across the Muslim world, fostering economic prosperity and cultural exchange. Governed by the Rashidun Caliphate, the region faces challenges in maintaining order and security amidst the influx of diverse populations. The defense of Islam and the protection of the holy sites remain paramount, shaping the region's political and military priorities.
