Prüm, is a town steeped in religious significance. A powerful Benedictine abbey dominates the landscape, its impressive buildings housing monks devoted to scholarship and prayer. Pilgrims wind their way through cobbled streets, seeking spiritual solace within the abbey walls. Workshops cater to the needs of the abbey and pilgrims, while surrounding villages provide food and resources. Life in Prüm revolves around faith, tradition, and the rhythm of the abbey's prayers.
