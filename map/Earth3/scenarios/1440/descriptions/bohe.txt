Bohemia reigns as a kingdom within the Holy Roman Empire, a land of central European grandeur. Prague, its jewel-toned capital, thrives with a renowned university and architectural wonders. Yet, religious tensions simmer. King Frederick III, recently crowned Holy Roman Emperor, pushes for Catholicism's return, challenging the Hussite movement advocating reform. Powerful nobles navigate this religious landscape, as silver mines continue to fuel Bohemia's wealth.
