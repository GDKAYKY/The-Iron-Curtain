Smolensk occupies a strategic position on the Dnieper River, a key city caught in a tug-of-war. Fortified and proud, it boasts a long history as a major trading center. However, its fate hangs in the balance, contested by both the Grand Duchy of Lithuania and the rising power of Muscovy. Lithuanian nobles currently hold sway, but tensions simmer as Muscovite forces press their claims. Smolensk's future hinges on the outcome of this power struggle, with its people caught in the middle. Despite the uncertainty, the city endures as a vital hub for trade and a symbol of resilience.
