Vitebsk stands as a crucial city within the Grand Duchy of Lithuania. Its strategic location on the Dvina River contributes to its economic prosperity through trade. A blend of Slavic and Baltic cultures shapes its identity. As a borderland city, Vitebsk plays a vital role in defending the duchy's western frontiers. Its fortifications and garrison contribute to the overall security of the region, while its commercial activity enhances the Grand Duchy's economic strength.
