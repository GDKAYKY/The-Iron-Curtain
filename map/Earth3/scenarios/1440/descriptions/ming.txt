The Ming Dynasty reigns supreme in 1440, its third emperor, the Zhengtong Emperor, recently restored to the throne after a power struggle. The dynasty grapples with internal reforms and external threats. Grand projects like the Forbidden City are underway, showcasing imperial power and cultural achievements. Trade flourishes along the Silk Road, bringing prosperity to merchants and enriching the capital. However, unrest simmers on the northern borders, demanding constant vigilance from the Ming military.
