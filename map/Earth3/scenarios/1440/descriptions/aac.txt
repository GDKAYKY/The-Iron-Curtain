Aachen, is a crossroads of power, pilgrimage, and prosperity. Imposing walls enclose a bustling city crowned by the magnificent Aachen Cathedral, a renowned pilgrimage site attracting pious visitors from across Europe. Skilled artisans produce everything from metalwork to textiles, supplying a vibrant marketplace. The Holy Roman Emperor is crowned here, imbuing the city with an air of political importance. Aachen thrives on a mix of religious fervor, imperial prestige, and a booming trade scene.
