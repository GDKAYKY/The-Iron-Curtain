A prosperous city on the shores of Lake Constance, where the Rhine River begins its journey. Imposing walls enclose a maze of narrow streets lined with merchants' houses. The mighty cathedral, a beacon of faith, dominates the skyline. Scholars gather at the renowned Council of Constance, debating theology and shaping the religious landscape. Fishermen cast their nets on the lake, while trade flourishes at the bustling harbor. Konstanz, a center of power and piety, thrives at the crossroads of commerce and ideas.
